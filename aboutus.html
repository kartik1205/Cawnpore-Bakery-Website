About Us

Cawnpore Bakery has been creating custom cakes and desserts long before it was the trendy thing to do. We started as a family bakeshop. Local customers fell in love. Word got out. Fast forward two generations and our artistry and quality continue to set the trend. Our experience is admired though not easily replicated. Over the years our business has grown to include a talented team of professional bakers, decorators and customer service ambassadors. Even still, at the heart of it all we are a family bakery dedicated to bringing you sweet memories
